dngn: el juego

You need a keyboard to play this in its current state.
You are the @ and the P is Pedro, the shopkeeper, who will chase you relentlessly as you try to steal his banana (the ananas).
The ananas is hidden in a box at random.
You move with the arrow keys.
Press spacebar or enter to check a box while standing on it.
The game ends when you find the ananas or when Pedro catches you.
Refreshing the page starts a new game and generates a new map.
